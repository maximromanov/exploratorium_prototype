Exploratorium

The page offers an exploratory insight into groups of individuals from the “History of Islām” (Taʾrīḫ al-islām) of al-Ḏahabī who share common ‘descriptors’ (sing. nisbaŧ). By selecting a ‘descriptor’ (), you can get preliminary analyses of geographical, chronological, and network patterns. The time frame can be selected by the following slider.


Space

The spatial pattern: the larger the dot, the more scholars who are qualified by the selected ‘descriptor’ are associated with the location.

Time


Chronological pattern: absolute and relative frequencies over time.


Based on the selected threshold for the frequencies, the nework of ‘descriptors’ will be modified. The colors represent three different categories: onomastic data (names), toponymic onomastic data (toponymic names), and toponymic data (toponyms). The graph is drawn based on the force-directed visualization and it is moving until a equilibrium is achieved.
